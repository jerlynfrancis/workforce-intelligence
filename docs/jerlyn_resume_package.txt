## 6. ATS OPTIMIZATION TIPS

### General Best Practices
1. **Use standard section headers:** Education, Experience, Skills, Certifications
2. **Avoid tables, text boxes, headers/footers** - ATS can't read them
3. **Use standard fonts:** Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman
4. **Save as .docx or PDF** (check job posting preference)
5. **Use keywords from job description** throughout resume
6. **Quantify achievements** with numbers, percentages, results
7. **Keep formatting simple** - no columns, graphics, or fancy designs

### Keywords to Include Based on Job Type

**For Data/Business Analyst Roles:**
- Data analysis, business intelligence, SQL, Python, R
- Power BI, Tableau, data visualization
- Statistical analysis, predictive modeling
- Business requirements, stakeholder management
- Agile, cross-functional collaboration
- KPIs, metrics, reporting
- Problem-solving, critical thinking

**For Retail Roles:**
- Customer service, customer experience
- Sales, upselling, cross-selling
- Cash handling, POS systems
- Merchandising, inventory management
- Team collaboration, team player
- Flexible availability, shift work
- Product knowledge, customer engagement
- Loss prevention, shrinkage control

### Tailoring Your Resume for Each Application

1. **Read the job description carefully** - highlight key requirements
2. **Mirror the language** - use same terms they use
3. **Prioritize relevant experience** - put most relevant roles first
4. **Adjust your summary** - align with specific role requirements
5. **Include relevant keywords** naturally throughout
6. **Remove irrelevant information** - keep it focused

### Common ATS Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Using headers/footers for contact information
❌ Using tables or text boxes
❌ Using images, logos, or graphics
❌ Using unusual fonts or heavy formatting
❌ Abbreviating without spelling out (use "Search Engine Optimization (SEO)")
❌ Using creative section titles (stick to standard ones)
❌ Submitting as image file or non-standard format
❌ Keyword stuffing (make it natural)

### Testing Your Resume

Before submitting:
1. Copy/paste into plain text document - does it still make sense?
2. Check for typos and formatting issues
3. Ensure dates are consistent (Month Year format)
4. Verify all contact information is correct
5. Remove any outdated information
6. Ask someone to review it
